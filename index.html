Background

Born on April 9, 2000, in Kobe City, Hyogo Prefecture.

April 2013: Started judo upon entering middle school.

April 2016: Joined the judo club of a high school known for producing Olympic athletes.

August 2018: Began studying seriously for the first time after my last judo match.

April 2020: Took a gap year but was admitted to Meiji University, a prestigious university in Japan.

August 2021: Pursued my dream of studying in the U.S.; left Meiji University and enrolled at Orange Coast College in California.

September 2023: Transferred to Knox College to start studying Computer Science.

To be continued.
